How Your Request Is Processed

A simplified real-world flow for insurance operations.

1. Application Submitted

Customer details and vehicle risk profile are captured with premium estimate logic.

2. Acknowledgement Triggered

System attempts email and SMS acknowledgement using configured provider credentials.

3. Underwriter Review

Authorized users can review, approve, or reject requests from the secure dashboard.

4. Policy PDF Generated

On approval, a policy number is assigned and a downloadable policy PDF is produced.
